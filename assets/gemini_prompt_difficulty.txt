AI 난이도 카드용 캐릭터 시트 (병아리 / 선수 / 타짜 / 신의손)

기존 20인 캐릭터 로스터(assets/gemini_prompt.txt)와는 별개의, 새게임 설정 화면의
"AI 난이도" 카드 4장 전용 신규 캐릭터. 2x2 격자 1장으로 생성한다.

---

A detailed digital illustration for a 2x2 character sheet grid, in the same clean,
precise digital illustration style as an existing Korean Hwatu (flower card) game
character roster (soft cel-shaded, semi-realistic proportions), set against a clean
white studio background. Each of the four cells depicts a completely new, original
character representing an escalating tier of go-stop (Korean card game) skill level.
All four characters share the identical pose across every cell: giving a thumbs-up
with one hand while holding a fanned set of detailed Hwatu cards (intricate floral
and character designs) in the other, looking directly at the camera.

Cell 1, top-left ('병아리' = Rookie/Chick, weakest tier): A very young, nervous-looking
novice player, school-age or early 20s, plain everyday clothes (hoodie or t-shirt),
fidgety posture, wide uncertain eyes, a slightly clumsy embarrassed smile. A tiny
yellow chick mascot sits on their shoulder.

Cell 2, top-right ('선수' = Player/Pro, mid tier): A confident, casually sharp club
regular in their late 20s-30s, sunglasses pushed up on the head or a slick jacket,
relaxed knowing smile, easy composed posture.

Cell 3, bottom-left ('타짜' = Card Shark, strong tier): A stylish, intense card
sharp in their 40s, sharp dark suit or a modernized hanbok-fusion outfit, narrow
focused eyes, faint confident smirk, subtle dramatic noir-style shadow/vignette —
evoking the classic Korean "Tazza" legendary gambler archetype. No cigarette or
alcohol imagery.

Cell 4, bottom-right ('신의손' = Hand of God, top tier): A legendary, almost
mythical ageless master, ornate traditional hanbok with gold trim, a faint golden
glow or soft particle sparkle around the hands, serene all-knowing expression,
calm godlike confidence.

All four characters keep the identical thumbs-up + fanned-Hwatu-cards pose and the
same clean white background, differing only in age, attire, expression, and aura to
visually convey the four skill tiers.
